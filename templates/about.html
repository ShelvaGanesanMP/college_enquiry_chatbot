P.A.S INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE OF SCIENCES

(Affiliated to Oxford,Approved by AICSTE New Delhi & IOSSR,New Delhi)

P.A.S International,College of Science and Technology (PASCST) was established in the year 1950-2024. Initially, it started functioning with Computer Science, Bio Sciences(Physics,Chemistry,Zoology,Botany), Information Science and Para Science branches, offering 3 years duration Undergraduate Degree Programme . A two years duration Masters Programme in Computer Science,Physics,Chemistry,Zoology & Botany where added in the year 2001-2024. where a 3 years duration degree programme in Science was introduced in the year 1950-1951. The approved intake for the courses are as follows; Computer Science & Technology: 60; Bsc Physics:60; Bsc Chemistry:60; Bsc Zoology:60; Bsc Botany:60; Msc Physics:40; Msc Chemistry:40; Msc Zoology:40; Msc Botany:40 with a total approved intake of 460 students per annum. All the programmes are affiliated to University Of Oxford USA, recognised by International Organisation for Study of Sciences and Research & All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. The course curriculum of these programmes are as per the norms and guidelines of the affiliating University viz.University of Oxford ,USA and all the relevant details about these programmes are available at www.universityofoxford.ac.in. ,Approved by AICSTE New Delhi & IOSSR New Delhi
