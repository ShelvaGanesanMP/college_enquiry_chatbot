Welcome To P.A.S International

P.A.S International,College of Science and Technology (PASCST) was established in the year 1950-2024. Initially, it started functioning with Computer Science, Bio Sciences(Physics,Chemistry,Zoology,Botany), Information Science and Para Science branches, offering 3 years duration Undergraduate Degree Programme . A two years duration Masters Programme in Computer Science,Physics,Chemistry,Zoology & Botany where added in the year 2001-2024. where a 3 years duration degree programme in Science was introduced in the year 1950-1951. All the programmes are affiliated to University Of Oxford USA, recognised by International Organisation for Study of Sciences and Research & All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi.
